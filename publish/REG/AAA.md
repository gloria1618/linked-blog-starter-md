Accumulated Adjustments Account
- acct to hold RE/EnP
- acct blnce goes up as money earned, down as money lost/withdrawn
- is the accum earnings/profit during yrs corp is an S corp
- prev undist income can be withdrawn tax free
	- so shareholder taxed on earned even if not dist, if dont dist then it sits in AAA
- AAA starts at 0
- distributions may not reduce AAA below 0. however AAA may be negative due to S crop loss/deductions

- inc to AAA
	- ord business income
	- sep stated inc/gain items (other than tax exempt income)
- dec to AAA
	- ord business loss
	- sep stated loss/deductions
	- nondeductible exp (other than exp related to tax exempt inc)
	- dist (may not reduce AAA below 0)
