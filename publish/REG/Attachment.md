Creation (Attachment) of the Security Interest
- attachment est right of a creditor in collateral vis-a-vis debtor
- gen, security interest will attach to an item of collateral if parties agree that security interest will attach
attachment is completed once all 3 req are satsified
- agmt
- creditor? gives value
- debtor has rights to collateral
agrmt can be
- in writing (incl electronic docs)
	- signed by debtor
- oral if secured party takes possession of collateral (aka pledge)
upon attachment, creditor is now a 'secured' creditor

Property in which Debtor Acquires Interest in Future (After Acquired Prop)
- secured party will sometimes will obtain security interest not only in debtor's present prop, but also in prop debtor will obtain in future (say worried about collateral losing value over time)
- permissible, facilitated by incl an 'after acquired prop clause' in security agrmt
- security interest attaches to after acq prop as soon as debtor acq interest in prop

Duties of Secured Party after Attachment
- secured party has duty to
	- file/send debtor termination stmt when debt paid
	- confirm for debtor unpaid amt left on secured debt
	- use reasonable care to preserve any collateral in secured party's possession
		- oral agrmnt ok
