- Audit Process
	- fed income tax sys based on self assessment of taxes
	- audit process ensures voluntary assessment + pmt of taxes is occurring 
	- return may be audited for variety of reasons, examination may take place in several ways
	- after audit, txpyr can agree w changes (if any) or can disagree/appeal decision
- Selecting Returns for Audit
	1. Stat Models (Discriminant Inv Function Sys DIF)
		- select returns most likely to contain errors, yield sig amts of additional tax revenue
	2. Random Selection
		- small # of returns manually selected
	3. Prior Yr Audit
		- if txpyr audited in prior yr
		- prior audit -> subs deficiency assessed, then subseq yr may be audited 
	4. Info Return Discrepancy
		- info forms, like W2/1099, don't match amts reported on return, may trigger audit 
	5. Deductions Exceeding Established Norms
		- indiv's itemized deductions are in excess of norms est for certain income lvls
- Audit Timing
	- most indiv returns audited w.in 2yrs from filing date
	- return may also be audited at any pt prior to expiration of statute of limitations 
		- varies, but no limit for fraud - vid
- Correspondence Audit (Rev for Math Errors)
	- as a result of IRS review of
		- info errors
			- eg incorrect SSN, missing signatures
		- matching issues
			- eg income reported on return doesnt match w2/1099
		- math errors
	- no need for formal IRS mtg
	- in case of errors, txpyr typ sent
		- revised computation
		- brief explanation of chng
		- bill of additional amt due/refund
	- no interest if req pmt timely
- Formal Examination (Office/Field Audit)
	- Office Audit
		- by IRS revenue agent, either in IRS office/via correspondence
		- used for indiv returns w few/no items of business income
	- Field Audit
		- by IRS rep, either at txpyr office/home/business place of txpyr's rep
- After Audit
	1. Issue Resolved
		- revenue agent can accept return, issue 'no chng report'
		- rev agent can recc certain chng + if txpyr agrees, txpyr signs Form 870 to waive right to appeal
	2. Unresolved Issues
		- agrmt cant be reached w indiv rev agent, txpyr receives copy of revenue agent's report + 30dy letter (preliminary notice) notif txpyr of right to [[Appeal]]
		- txpyr has 30dys to request admin appeal w appeals officer (not a Court of Appeals)
		- pretty sure this is appeals conference 
	3. Fast Track Remediation
		- avail for small business owners, SE indiv
		- mediator from IRS Office of Appeals assigned, goal: resolution in 60dys
		- still unresolved, normal appeals process is still avail
