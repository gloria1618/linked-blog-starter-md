Surety's Rights Against Principal Debtor
- Exoneration (Suit to Compel Pmt) - *before* surety pays creditor
	- principal debtor owes surety a duty to perform
	- if principal fails to pay creditor, surety may bring a suit for exoneration in equity to compel principal to pay
- Subrogation (Enforcement of Creditor's Right against Principal) 
	- after paying principal's debtor obligation, surety may enforce any rights creditor had against principal debtor
	- incl right to enforcement of any security interest and any priority in bankruptcy that creditor had
- Reimbursement/Indemnification (Suit against Principal after Pmt)
	- surety entitled to reimbursement from principal debtor for any amt surety paid on behalf of debtor
		- aka right to 'indemnification'
