Prop Excl
- money debtor earns after petition filed (post petition earnings)
	- so like ur salary
- basic household items needed to live (clothing, med devices, like)

Trustee is Hypothetical Lien Creditor as of Filing Date
- trustee treated as having lien on all of debtor's prop the instant bankruptcy petition filed
- trustee has priority over all creditors except creditors w prior perfected security interests, or prior statutory/judicial liens (stat, mechanic, artisans,contractors)

Power over Fraudulent Transfer
- trustee has power to set aside fraudulent transfers made w/in 2yrs of filing date
- fraudulent transfer
	- any transfer made w intent to hinder/delay/defraud creditors
	- any transfer where debtor rec less than equivalent value while debtor insolvent

Trustee can Disaffirm Preferences:
- debtor doesn't get to choose who, how much, and order paid
- preference is transfer made to particular creditor that debtor wants to give preference to
	- court will look at all transfers made to creditors w/in 90dys prior to filing petition 
		- 1yr if creditor is an insider, such as officer of debtor org or close relative 
	- court has power to set aside transfer and take back from creditor so it becomes part of bankruptcy estate
:Preferential Pmt *exam important*
- transfer made to/for benefit of creditor (accelerating/prepay)
- on acct of an antecedent debt (ie alr existing) of debtor
	- pmt preference only if for antecedent (preexisting debt)
	- contemporaneous exchange for new value is not preference
		- ![[Pasted image 20260113233821.png]]
- made w/in 90dys prior to filing of petition (1yr if insider)
- made while debtor insolvent
- results in creditor receiving more than creditor would have rec under Bankruptcy Code
