Est Taxes (Req Min)
- txpyr req to make est quarterly tax pmts if both of following met:
	- amt of taxes owed (excess of tax L over withholding) is expected to be 1k+
	- txpyrs withholding is less than lesser of (safe harbor)
		- 90% of current yr's tax or
		- 100% of last yr's tax
- applies even if indiv files tax return w 0 tax L in prior yr
- exception: txpyr had AGI in excess of150k (75k MFS) in prior yr, 110% of prior yr's tax L is used to compute safe harbor for est pmts

Failure to pay est taxes (Penalty)
- no penalty due under an circumstances if balance of tax owed at filing is under 1k
- IRS may waive penalty if failure to pay was result of 
	- casualty
	- disaster
	- illness
	- death of txpyr
