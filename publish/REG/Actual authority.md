Actual Authority
- aka 'real authority', 'authority'
- authority agent reasonably believes they possess bc of principal's comms to agent (oral/written)
- agent w actual authority has power and right to bind principal to contracts w 3rd party
- express actual authority
	- oral/written
	- incl all powers principal expressly grants w/in 'four corners' of agency agrmt
- implied actual authority
	- authority agent could reasonably believe is implied along w express grant
	- incl authority to do things reasonably necessary to carry out agency

Termination of Actual Authority
- termination of actual authority can occur by
	1. Act of the Parties: agent quit/fired
		-  termination of an agency can occur thru acts of either
			- principal (revocation)
			- agent (renunciation)
		- if termination violates parties' contract, damages could be available
		- if agency coupled w interest, only agent has power to terminate agency relationship
	2. Accomplishment of Obj/Expiration of Stated Period
		- if agency for limited purpose, actual authority terminated when obj accomplished
		- if agency for stated period, actual authority terminated upon expiration of the period
		- if no time sated, terminates after reasonable time
	3. Automatic Termination of Actual Authority (by operation of law): BID LID
		- terminates both actual n apparent authority
		- notice to 3rd parties not req when agency relationship is terminated by law
		- BIDLID
			- discharge in Bankruptcy of *principal*
			- Incapacity of *principal*
			- Death of either 
				- death of principal doesn't terminate agency until agent has notice of principal's death
			- failure to acquire necessary License
			- subsequent Illegality
			- Destruction of subj matter of agency
