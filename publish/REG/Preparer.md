Tax Return Preparer
[[Compliance.Uneth Penalties]]

- Tax Return Preparer
	- any person prepping/employs other ppl to prep for compensation any req fed tax return/claim for refund
	- any tax prof w PTIN is auth to prep fed tax returns
		- annual filing szn program participants, PTIN holders
		- Unlimited Representation Rights: Tax Practitioners (Licensed)
			- enrolled agents, CPA, attorneys 
			- may represent clients on any matters incl audits, pmt/collection issues, appeals
		- PTIN Holders w No Credentials: Tax Preparers (No License)
			- PTIN holders who dont have prof credential and don't participate in annual filing szn prgrm are auth only to prep tax returns
			- gen no authority to rep clients before IRS
	- CPA license not req to be tax return preparer 
- PTIN irs tax id #
	- IRS.gov to get PTIN
	- all paid tax return preparers req to register w IRS, obtain PTIN
- NOT a tax return preparer
	- merely furnishes typing, reproducing, other mechanical assistance (data entry, solicit client info, copies, mailing)
	- prep return/refund claim for employer
	- prep return/refund claim as fiduciary (trustee, executor)
- Signing v Nonsigning Tax Return Preparer 
	- Signing
		- whoever has primary resp for overall accuracy of return/claim
	- Nonsigning
		- anyone who is not a signing tx return preparer, but prepares subs portion of return/offers advice
		- assist in prep, work is not merely mechanical
