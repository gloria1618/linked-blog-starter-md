Secured transactions - collateral can be personal prop such as furniture, jewelry, inv, equipment, AR, etc
- debtor buys smthn from creditor/secured party on credit or borrows $ from a creditor
- creditor wants to be able to rely on smthn (a security interest) other than the debtors promise to ensure pmt
Security Interest
- limited right in specific personal prop (collateral) of debtor that allows creditor to take prop (repossessing) if debtor fails to fulfill credit obligation
- if collateral is real estate, mortgage laws apply

[[Attachment]]: Step 1 creditor v debtor
- security interest is effective bw parties as soon as certain steps taken to attach interest
- once interest attaches, if debtor defaults, creditor has some right to take collateral from debtor to satisfy debt
- attachment doesnt provide creditor w rights against 3rd parties (other creditors) who might also have interest in collateral
[[Perfection]]: Step 2 creditor v 3rd parties
- to gain rights over 3rd parties, creditor must take added steps to perfect security interest
- perfection basically serves as form of notice creditor has a security interest in collateral, and bc of notice, gives creditor rights in collateral superior to certain 3rd parties

Scope of Article 9 of [[UCC]]
- applicable w certain exceptions
	- most contractual security interests in persona prop or fixtures (personal prop so attached to real prop as to become part of the real prop), and
	- outright sales of AR
- not applicable
	- security interest in land (mortgage)
	- wage claims (garnishment)
	- statutory liens, ex mechanics liens

Purchase Money Security Interest (PMSI)
- PMSI is special type of security interest that has priority over all other types of security interests in same collateral, if PMSI is properly perfected (ensures creditors are in favored position)
- PMSI arises when
	- creditor sells collateral to debtor on credit, retaining security interest for purchase price, or
	- creditor adv funds used by debtor to purchase collateral
	- * notice creditor may, but need not, be seller of collateral
