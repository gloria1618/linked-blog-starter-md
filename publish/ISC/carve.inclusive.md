- SOC1
	- considered subserv org if
		- serv prov are likely relevant to UE IC over fin reporting
		- CSOC - ctrls implemented at subserv necessary to achieve ctrl obj in mgmt sys descrip
- SOC 2/3
	- considered subserv only if
		- serv prov relevant to report users' understanding of servorg sys as it relates to applic TSC
		- CSOC - ctrls at subserv necessary, in combo w servorg ctrls, to prov ra servcomm/sysreq achieved
- a subserv org
	- may be sep entity ext to serv org
	- may be related entity, like subsidiary of parent company that owns servorg
- servorg mgmt resp for det if it uses subserv
	- if yes, mgmt resp for det to carve out/incl subserv ctrls w.in engagement scope

- Carve out
	- addresses services prov by subserv in which CSOCs excl
		- from description of servorg sys
		- from engagement scope
	- IDs
		1. nature of services by subserv
		2. ctrl types expected to be performed at subserv that are needed, in combo w ctrl at servorg, to prov ra ctrl obj in mgmt sys descrip (SOC1) or servorg servcomm/sysreq (SOC2) achieved
			- logical AC, ctrls relevant to completeness/accuracy of processing transactions or ctrls relevant accurate/complete reporting
		3. ctrls at servorg used to monitor effectiveness of the subserv org ctrl
	- practical when
		- inclusive challenges sufficiently onerous, not practical
		- serv auditor not indep of subserv
		- Type1/2 service auditor report on subserv, meeting user needs, available
		- servorg cant obtain contractual/other commitment from subserv abt willingness to be incl in SOC2 engagement
	- if subserv services/ctrl pervasive effect on servorg sys, cant do carve out
		- may result in descrip so limited its useless for users
		- factors
			- sig of portion of sys func performed by subserv
			- complexity of service/types of ctrls expected to be implemented by subserv
			- extent to which achievement of servorg servcomm/sysreq based on applic TSC depends on subserv ctrls
			-  # of applic TSC that wouldn't be met if ctrl types expected to be implemented at subserv aren't
			- ability of intended users of report to obtain sufficient approp evidence about design, oe (t2), of ctrls at subserv
- Inclusive 
	- addresses services prov by subserv in which descrip of servorg sys incl description of
		1. nature of services by subserv
		2. parts of subserv sys to prov services, incl ctrls needed, in combo w servorg ctrls, to prov ra ctrl obj in mgmt sys descrip (SOC1) or servorg servcomm/sysreq (SOC2) achieved
	- gen prov more info for report users, most useful in following
		- prov services extensive
		- T1/2 report that meets need of report users not available from subserv
		- info about subserv not readily available from other sources
	- serv auditor must be independent of both entities, engagement covers both

- mult subserv, may prep description using
	- carve out for 1+
	- inclusive for rest
