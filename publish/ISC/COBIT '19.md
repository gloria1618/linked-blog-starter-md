COBIT dist bw governance/mgmt, recog as 2 disciplines existing for diff reasons, req diff set of org resources
- Governance
	- resp of BoD
		- consisting of chairperson + focused org structures (audit/exec/mktg committee)
	- Governance Stakeholders
		- internal stakeholders
			- incl BoD, mgmt, business/IT managers, assurance providers, risk managers
		- external stakeholders
			- regulators, investors, business partners, IT vendors, parties entitled to info about compliance/risk mitigation but not entitled to same info as internal stakeholders
- Mgmt
	- select/guided by BoD
	- resp for daily planning/admin of op
	- consists of
		- CEO
		- CFO
		- COO
		- CIO, CTO, CSO, CPO, CDO - diff for diff org
