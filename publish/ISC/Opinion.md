Forming Opinion in SOC Engagement
- when forming opinion, auditor should eval
	- sufficiency, approp of obtained evidence
	- whether uncorrected misstmts material, indiv/agg
- forms opinion abt if
	- subj matter in accord w/based on the criteria, in all material respects
	- assertion fairly stated in all material respects
- opinion focuses on
	- fair presentation of mgmts descrip of sys
	- suitability of design of ctrls related to ctrl obj stated in mgmt descrip
	- effective op of ctrls stated in mgmt descrip (Type 2 only)
- overall obj of SOC 1/2 consistent
	- subj matter for which opinion formed is diff
		- SOC1 - regarding ctrls relevant to user entities IC over fin reporting
		- SOC2 - ctrls relelvant to TSC

Opinions

- Unmodified/unqualified
	- in all material respects, based on crit described in mgmts assertion
		1. mgmts descrip fairly presents sys that was design/implemented
			- SOC1 fairly
			- SOC2  in accord w descrip crit
		2. ctrls in descrip suitably designed

			- SOC2 ctrls ... to prov reasonable assurance that service commitments/sys req achieved based on TSC
		3. ctrls in descrip op effectively (Type2 only)
			- same as 2.
	- CUEC

		- if application of Complementary Subservice Org Ctrls needed to achieve related ctrl obj in mgmt descrip of sys, stmt to that effect has been made
- Qualified
	- except for effects of matters > modification, descrip presented in accord w descrip crit + ctrls suitably designed, op effectively (T2) in all material respects
	- SOC1
		- mistmts in mgmt descrip/deficiencies in design/oe(t2) of ctrls
			- limited to 1+, but not all, aspects of descrip of sys/ctrl obj
			- dont affect opinion on other aspects of descrip of sys/ctrl obj
		- changes
			- sep p#, before opinion p#, prov descrip of matters giving rise to modification
			- opinion p#: in our opinion, except for matter referred to in preceding p#, IAMR, based on crit desc in assertion in section2
	- SOC2

			- deficiences in d/oe(t2) of ctrls material but not pervasive
		- scope limitation

- Adverse
	- descrip misstmts (indiv/agg) material + pervasive (throut descrip)
	- or deficiencies in design/op material + pervasive (across all/most ctrl obj)
	- SOC1
		- sep p# before opinion, prov descrip of matters leading to mod
		- opinion p#: in opinion, bc of matters referred to in preceding p#, IAMR, based on crit described in __ assertion in section 2:
			- descrip does not fairly present {sys} designed n implemented thruout period
			- ctrls related to ctrl obj stated in descrip not sd to prov ra ctrl obj would be achieved if ctrl oe thruout period {date} to {date}
			- ctrls tested, which were necessary to prov ra ctrl obj in descrip achieved, did not oe thruout period {date} to {date}
	- SOC2
		- + or if prevent achievement of all/most of servcomm/sysreq based on aTSC
		- serv auditor resp p#: evidence obtained sufficient/approp for adverse opinion
		- sep p# before opinion, prov descrip of matters leading to mod
		- opinion p#: in opinion, bc of significance of matter(s) in preceding p#, IAMR:
			- descrip of {sys} does not present sys that was designed n implemented thruout period {date} to {date} in accord w descrip crit
			- ctrls in descrip not sd thruout period {date} to {date} to prov ra servcomm/sysreq achieved based on aTSC
			- ctrls in descrip did not oe thruout period {date} to {date} to prov ra servcomm/sysreq achieved based on aTSC
