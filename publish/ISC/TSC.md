Applicability of TSC to SOC2/3 Engagements
- due to nature of sys of IC, entities face certain risk threatening ability to achieve their obj
- due to inherent risks, entities resp for
	- design/implement suitable ctrls to mitigate risks
- AICPA's ASEC has est TSC setting forth outcomes that ctrls should meet to acheive entity's unique obj
	- enables practitioners to eval/report on ctrls over CAPPS (indiv/in combo)
		- Confidentiality
			- info designated as confidential protected to meet entity's obj
		- Availability
			- info/sys available for op, used to meet obj
		-  Processing Integrity
			- sys processing complete, valid, accurate, timely, auth to meet obj
		- Privacy
			- personal info collect, use, retain, disclose, disposed of to meet obj
		- Security
			- info/sys protected against unauth access/disclosure/sys damage compromising other 4 crit n affect ability to meet obj
- TSC designed to prov flexibility in
	- application
	- use for a variety of diff subj matters
		- SOC for cybersec 
			- effectiveness of ctrls w.in entity's cybersec risk mgmt program to achieve cybersec obj using TSC relevant to
				- sec
				- availability
				- confidentiality as a ctrl crit
		- SOC2
			- Type 1
				- same subj matter as Type2 but doesnt contain
					- opinion on op effectiveness of ctrls
					- detailed descrip of ToC performed, results
			- Type 2
				- suitability of design + op effectiveness of ctrls incl in mgmt descrip of sys relevant to 1+ TSC thruout specified period to achieve obj based on those crit
				- incl opinion on op effectiveness + descrip of ToC, results
		- SOC3
			- design + op effectiveness of ctrls over sys relevant to 1+ TSC
			- contain opinion on op effectiveness of ctrls but not descrip of ToC, results
		- suitability of design + op effectiveness of ctrls of an entity, other than servorg, over 1+ sys relevant to 1+ TSC
			- ex: SOC SC engagement
		- suitability of design of ctrls over TSC to achieve entity's obj based on related TSC
