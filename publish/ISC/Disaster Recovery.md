Disaster recovery - consists of plan for restoring/cont IT func in event of destruction of not only program/data files, but also computer processing capability 
- ST prob/outage /=/ disaster
- if processing cannot be quickly reest at original processing site, then disaster recovery needed
- steps in disaster recovery plan 
	1. assess risks
	2. id mission critical apps/data
	3. develop plan for handling mission critical apps
	4. det resp of personnel involved in disaster recovery
	5. test disaster recovery plan
- alt processing facilities (all have connections+off site)
	- cold site
		- all elec connections, other phys req for data processing
		- no actual equipment
		- usually 1-3dys to be op
		- normally generic hardware that can be readily/quickly obtained from hardware vendors
		- cheapest
	- hot site
		- equipped to take over data processing
		- prewired, incl hardware, office equip to perform functions
		- backup copies of essential data file/programs maybe maintained at loc/nearby
		- expensive
	- warm site
		- alr hardware installed
		- falls short of hot site processing capabilities/actual business during normal ops due to lack of fully op computer/office equip
		- 0-3dys
